Instruction (1/3)

In this experiment, you will see many faces and your task is to compare and judge face similarities.

There are two types of tasks: face pair task and face triplet task

Face pair task

Rate the similarity levek of the face pair from 1 to 7.

1 -- Maximally dissimilar, 7 -- Maximally similar

Face triplet task

Decide which of the bottom faces is more similar to the top one by pressing left or right button
