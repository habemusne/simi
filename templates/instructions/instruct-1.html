Instruction (1/3)

In this experiment, you will see many faces and your task is to compare and judge face similarities.

There are two types of tasks: face pair task and face triplet task

Face Pair Task

Rate the similarity level of the face pair from 1 to 7.

1 -- Maximally dissimilar, 7 -- Maximally similar

Face Triplet Task

Decide which of the bottom face images is more similar to the top one. You can press "Left"/ "Right" button or click on the left/ right face image to indicate your choice.
